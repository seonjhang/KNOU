(3) Divide age into four groups: “20-30”, “31-40”, “41-50”, and “50+”, and find a table of division with diabetes. Also draw overlapping bars, compare and explain the distribution.

Spliting age into four groups

: A new derivative variable must be created in order for age to be divided into continuous variables and randomly grouped categories.

#age_category란 파생변수 만들기
data$age_category <- ifelse(data$age>=20 & data$age<=30, "20-30", ifelse(data$age>30 & data$age<=40, "31-40", ifelse(data$age>40 & data$age<=50, "41-50", "50+")))
